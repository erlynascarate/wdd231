What This App Does
This web application helps users discover activities based on their current mood. Whether you feel happy, stressed, or bored, the app suggests useful and enjoyable ideas to improve your day.

How to Use It

Select your mood using the available filter options.
Browse the list of recommended activities.
Click on an activity to see more details.
Save your favorite activities to access them later.

Benefits

Helps you decide what to do when you feel bored or unmotivated.
Encourages positive habits and productivity.
Provides quick and simple ideas for different moods.
Improves your daily routine in an easy way.
